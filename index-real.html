Features

Everything your music library needs

TrackTool puts professional-grade music management tools in a clean, fast interface built for Windows.

🎵
Audio Fingerprinting

Identify unknown or mislabeled tracks using AcoustID and MusicBrainz. Automatically fill in the correct title, artist, album, year, and genre.

✏️
Metadata Editor

Edit title, artist, album, track number, genre, year, composer, comments, and album art. Save to disk or just for the current session.

⚡
Batch Processing

Fix metadata on your entire library at once. TrackTool processes tracks in parallel — even large folders load fast.

▶️
Built-in Player

Play your tracks directly in TrackTool. Skip silence, auto-advance, scrub, and control volume — no separate app needed.

Track Leveling

Automatically normalizes track volumes to ~-14 LUFS broadcast standard so you never have to reach for the volume knob between songs.

🔁
Duplicate Detection

Automatically detects duplicate tracks in your list and lets you review and remove them with a single click.

📊
Stats & Export

See total tracks, playtime, top artists, albums, and genres. Export your track list as CSV or TXT for use in other apps.

📂
Drag & Drop Loading

Drop files and folders directly onto TrackTool. Load single files, entire folders, or add folders to an existing list.

🔍
Search & Filter

Instantly filter your track list by title, artist, album, or filename. Click stats to filter by artist, album, or genre.
